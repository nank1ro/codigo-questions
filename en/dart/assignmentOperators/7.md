# --instructions--

What does `x %= 4` do when `x` is `9`?

# --answers--

- Sets `x` to the remainder of `9 / 4`, which is `1`
- Sets `x` to the result of `9 / 4`, which is `2.25`
- Sets `x` to `4`
- Sets `x` to `36`
